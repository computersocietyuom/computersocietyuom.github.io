About

What is IEEE ?

The Institute of Electrical and Electronic Engineers is the world's largest technical professional organization dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity.

The organization inspires a global community to innovate for a better tomorrow through its more than 423,000 members in over 160 countries. Also its highly cited publications, conferences, technology standards and other professional and educational activities gives wings for the upcoming generations to fly.

IEEE is the trusted “voice” for engineering, computing and other technology information around the globe.

What is IEEE CS ?

IEEE Computer Society is a professional society of the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers. Its purpose and scope is "to advance the theory, practice, and application of computer and information processing science and technology" and the "professional standing of its members."

What is IEEE in Sri Lanka Section ?

IEEE Sri Lanka Section has been established to foster technological innovation and excellence for the benefit of humanity and advancement of technical professionalism. IEEE Sri Lanka section was endorsed 12 years ago on 14th November 2003 under IEEE Region 10.

Currently, the section has a community of over 2000 members.

IEEE Sri Lanka Section comprises of University academic staff members, industry professionals and university students across the country. Youth Membership of the organization is represent two streams as Students Branches and Young Professionals (YP). Women in Engineering (WIE) is another major affinity group that made for women involved in the Engineering profession.

What is IEEE student branch of University of Moratuwa ?

The IEEE University of Moratuwa student branch strives to create an atmosphere of technology for the upcoming engineers of the University of Moratuwa.
